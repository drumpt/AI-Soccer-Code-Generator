There are currently 3 possible state options:
1) Relative Position: relative position of active robot in relation to the ball, orientation and orientation error
2) Full Position: relative position and velocity of the all robots in relation to the ball, orientation and orientation error of active robot
3) Minimap: minimap with robot positions and active robot position and orientation. (currently not available)
There are currently 3 possible action options:
1) 7 Discrete: seven discrete actions defined as a ratio over the maximum linear velocity of the robot
2) 13 Discrete: thirteen discrete actions defined as a ratio over the maximum linear velocity of the robot
3) Continous: continuous actions for the wheel and slider actions (applied to DDPG algorithm)
There are currently 3 possible action options:
1) Positive Follow Ball: positive reward in relation to distance and orientation difference to the ball
2) Negative Follow Ball: negative reward in relation to distance and orientation difference to the ball
3) Basic: score/concede reward, distance between robots and the ball, distance between ball and opponent goal
4) Differential: score/concede reward, differential reward based on ball movement
5) Checkpoint: reward is gained/lost if the ball is moving to/from the opponent field
